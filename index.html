Hi, I am Julius

Most of my childhood I spent in the wonderful city of Munich. From age 8 to 10 I attended the German school of Washington D.C. as my family moved there for a brief work stint of my father. In 2009, I graduated from a public school in the city center, conveniently located right next to the Octoberfest.

I grew up playing a ton of sports, including fencing, mountain-biking, climbing, and tennis. Additionally I played the piano. My biggest passions in my teenage years however were football (the European kind), playing video games (Warcraft III, Total War Series, AoE II, Battlefield, Elder Scrolls, Gothic, etc.) and reading books (mainly historical and fiction).

Munich is an amazing city that I can recommend anyone to visit. Even though I have been away for a long time, it is still the place that I call home.
